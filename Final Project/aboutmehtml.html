About the Creators:


Anusha Palanivelu

Anusha Palanivelu, a 16 year old living in San Ramon, has a talent in voicing
her unique and thoughful opinions, motivated by her remarkable interest to
interact with her peers. She contributed greatly to this project through her
in-depth research on the presidential candidates, following with presenting the
various views on policies and helping write the game script. Anusha was introduced
to the world of code from the event Hackathon, eager to manipulate something
to turn it into something new. She joined Girls Who Code and is really grateful
for the experience she recieve on the exposure for coding.


“Try everything you never know what could happen” - Anusha
