The Game

Our Purpose for this game is to deliver raw information for our audience with which
they can form their opinions without being hindered by the biased outlooks on political issues.




How To Play:
The game is set up in a form of a debate.
The player will be able to choose the different
political views they would like to be educated
on, but ultimately, the player will also have to
understand counter arguments on each political view.
The information provided were created through an
unbiased research process.
Objective:
The objective of the game is for the players
to be educated on the current issues surrounding
the presidential candidates of the upcoming
2020 election. Through a educational visual novel,
the player will able to form their own opinion
without being hindered back by biased opinions.
